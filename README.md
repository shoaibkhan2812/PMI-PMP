# PMI-PMP

* Class 1
------

Fill up the application on pmi.org. Your application may be selected for an audit. This is totally random.

You can track the status of application via your dashboard from pmi.org. Once the application is accepted, You'd be asked to pay the fee.
Once payment is made, a serial number would be generated and using the same, exam can be scheduled. Dates for exam can be schedule 365 days a year. The exam needs to be attempted within one year from the date of acceptance of the application.

A PMP credential is valid for 3 years.

Once certified, the membership may be renewed after 3 years. For renewal, a credit of 60 pdu is required.

1 hour = 1 PDU (Professional Development Unit)
One can watch webinars in pmi.org and get these PDUs. These are called CCR(Certificate Continuing Requirements).
The PDUs will be updated on the dashboard.

* PMP Exam format

- 50% agile and 50% waterfall model.
- **180 questions**, 5 questions do no carry marks. __*No Negative Marking*__
- **Time: 230 mins** (Two optional 10 mins break).
- One cannot go back to the questions attempted before the break.
- Do not spend more than 1 min per question.
